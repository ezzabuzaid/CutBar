# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on ideas and technical outcomes, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Accept feedback and correct mistakes quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions, reviews, or collaboration.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, reviews, and related community discussions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness misconduct, contact the maintainer privately through GitHub.
Include links and context so the report can be investigated.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may result in a permanent ban from project collaboration spaces.
